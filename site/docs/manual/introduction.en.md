### Introduction

[Ant Design Charts](https://github.com/ant-design/ant-design-charts) is the React version of [AntV](https://antv.vision), more friendly to React developers, <b>developed by the same team</b>.

- Out of the box: Presents high-quality charts by default, incorporating research on developer experience and user experience into the default chart configuration

- Easy to configure: Users can easily adjust chart details according to specific business needs

- Great experience: Visual and interactive experience focuses on the essential function of charts - **displaying and discovering information**

<br /> Generate charts like using components, out of the box, you don't even need to modify any configuration items to meet your needs, truly good by default. Are you excited to try it?
